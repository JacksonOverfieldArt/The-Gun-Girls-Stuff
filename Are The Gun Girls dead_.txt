Are The Gun Girls dead?

The only two Gun Girls members who have died are leader Vanessa Jones, who died of drug overdose at age 63 in 2004, and youngest Lesbian tomboy member Winona Townsend who died of cancer at age 56 in 2015. As of 2021, The only five surviving members of The Gun Girls are Lori Vandercamp, Jenny Sparks, Scarlett Williams, and Sammie Isacson. The group officially discontinued in 2004 after Vanessa's death.
